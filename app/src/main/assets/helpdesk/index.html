  • Semester Registration

  • How can I register or enrol for a semester?

    Ans. For you to register you must have paid or pay registration fee. Click the Home button on your app. Click the Registration button. Click Semester Registration button. Fill all the form fields. Click the Register button if you wish to proceed with the registration or Click the Cancel button to terminate

  • Im failing to register for Semester Registration?

    Ans. There is a problem with your account please email details and the error you have encountered with subject Semester Registration. trust@trustacademy.ac.zw or exams@trustacademy.ac.zw

  • Exam Registration

  • How to get over a heart break?

    Ans. For you to register you must have paid up or have a payment plan. Click the Home button on your app. Click the Registration button. Click Exam Registration button. Fill all the form fields. Click the Register button if you wish to proceed with the registration or Click the Cancel button to terminate.

  • I’m failing to register for Exams?

    Ans. There is a problem with your account please email details and the error you have encountered with subject Exam Registration. admin@trustacademy.ac.zw or exams@trustacademy.ac.zw

  • Payments

  • How can pay my Fees?

    Ans. Click the Home button on your app. Click Payments button, Select payment method of your choice. If you select Bank Transfer (Bank Account 123456789), please make sure you fill all the form fields and enter your Student ID on the REF field. If you select Merchant (Merchant Code 61326), please make sure you fill all the form fields and keep the confirmation message.

  • How can I check my fees?

    Ans. Click the Home button on your app. Click the Account button. The app will display all information pertaining to the register user. More information and queries on payments email with subject “payment issues”. sumbururu@trustacademy.ac.zw or kanengoni@trustacademy.ac.zw

  • How can I apply for a payment plan?

    For you to apply for a payment plan you must have paid any amount first. Click the Home button on your app. Click the Payments button. Click Payment Plan button. Fill all the form fields, select the interest rate of your choice (radio button). Click the Apply button if you wish to proceed with the application or Click the Cancel button to terminate. Payment Plans will be approved by the admin and a confirmation message will be sent to the student email address. More information on payments email.
